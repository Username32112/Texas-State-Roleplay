Texas State Roleplay | Staff Application

Welcome to the Texas State Roleplay Staff Application! We're excited that you're interested in joining our staff team. Before submitting your application, please ensure you meet all of the requirements below.

Requirements

  • You must provide accurate and honest information throughout the application.
  • You must be 13 years of age or older.
  • You must have no more than 5 active moderations on your Roblox account.
  • Safe Chat must be disabled on your Roblox account.
  • You must complete this application using a computer or laptop.
  • You must demonstrate strong spelling, punctuation, and grammar (SPaG).
  • You must be a member of the Texas State Roleplay Discord server.
  • You must remain professional, respectful, and mature at all times.
  • You must follow all server rules, staff policies, and management decisions.
  • You must not share or copy application questions or answers.
  • Low-effort, joke, AI-generated, or copied responses may result in denial.
  • Incomplete applications will not be reviewed.

Before You Begin

Take your time and answer every question to the best of your ability. Detailed, thoughtful responses have a much better chance of being accepted than short or rushed answers.

Good luck with your application, and thank you for your interest in becoming a member of the Texas State Roleplay Staff Team!
